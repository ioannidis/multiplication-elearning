Student Help

Lessons

Lessons list

Once a student is connected to the application, he immediately goes to the "Dashboard" where all the lessons are presented.

The courses consist of:

  • The number zero, where the theory of multiplication by 0 is taught.
  • The numbers from 1 to 10, where the relevant theory is presented and a small evaluation test is performed.
  • Review 1 which is a test on numbers 1, 2, 3.
  • Review 2 which is a test on numbers 4, 5, 6.
  • Review 3 which is a test on numbers 7, 8, 9.
  • The final review which is the final test and includes questions on all numbers.
Teaching process

The teaching of each lesson consists of theory and examination with a mini review test. The students can read the content that is provided by their teacher, can read the multiplication table and then they can watch the education video.

After the student finishes with the theory, they can click on the “Mini Review” tab to start the test and evaluate the knowledge they have received!

Student performance evaluation

There are three levels for student assessment:

  1. 100 is considered the ultimate excellent!
  2. Degree greater than or equal to 95 (Excellent)
  3. Degree greater than or equal to 75 and less than 95 (Good)
  4. Degree less than 75 (Failure)
Gradual evaluation

As shown in the following figures, the lessons are locked, and in order to unlock the next lessons, all the previous ones must have been completed, ie the student must have been evaluated with a grade grater than 75. The currently available course is shown in blue, while the ones in gray are locked.

Mini reviews

The small tests, after each number, consist of 6 questions.

Review

Each review refers to the previous three numbers from it. For example, review 1 is for the numbers 1, 2, 3.

Final review

The final review includes questions for all numbers, from 1 to 10. It includes 24 questions.

Result

At the end of each review test, the student's grade is presented, as well as the results of each question.

Reward system

To motivate students to perform better, the application uses a reward system. More specifically, the indirect goal of the students is to collect all 17 animals! Each animal unlocks only when the student scores with excellent (> = 95) in the corresponding lesson!
